Necessary herring gull facts:

  • Common Name: Herring Gull
  • Scientific Name: Larus smithsonianus
  • Order: Charadriiformes
  • Family: Laridae

Ah yes, herring gulls, a classic bird.

Most "seagulls" you've seen are likely herring gulls. They're everywhere, very identifiable, and big.

I admire their attitude.
