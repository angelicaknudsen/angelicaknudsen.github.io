Modular Water Collection System

Timeline: February-May 2023

Objective

Proof of concept part of the class 2.00A (Designing for the Future: Earth, Sea, and Space). Aim of design was to optimize water collection for marine research. Worked with other students to design a modular system with detachable test tubes and a peristaltic pump that turns and dispenses water from a flexible tube.

Outcomes & Contributions

Cut about 5-10 minutes spent necessary for viewing data from each file, as some files had about 10+ parameters

Technical Details & Skills

CAD: Autodesk Fusion 360, OnShape
