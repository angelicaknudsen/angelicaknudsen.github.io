Objective

This project is done as part of MIT Wind, a competitor in the US Department of Energy's 2026 Collegiate Wind Competition in the northeast region. The competition has three main components that contribute to your team's overall score: turbine design, turbine performance, and project development+connection creation.

As build lead of MIT Wind, I lead the mechanical and electrical subteams on the design and testing of the turbine. The designs presented here are preliminary and will be iterated in the coming months.
